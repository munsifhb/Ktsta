About

brief history of ktsta

Katsina state transport Authority was inherited from Kaduna State transport authority when the state was created in 1987 by the military administration of General Ibrahim Badamasi Babangida Although the organisation was shut down in 1990 by the then State military administrator Col. Yahaya J. Madaki but was however re-established in 1992 by the first executive Governor of Katsina State Alhaji Sa’idu Barda through the advice of Rt. Hon. Aminu Bello Masari CFR who was then Commissioner of Works Housing and Transport.

mission

Our goal is to provide safe, affordable, and comfortable transportation services to our customers across the country and beyond, without compromising safety, by utilising quality personnel and resources and by providing cost-effective, sustainable, and problem-solving solutions that help our customers achieve their goals.

vsssion

To be the most visible mass transit authority in the country and beyond by providing dependable, affordable, comfortable, safe, and sustainable transportation services through best practises and ethical processes.
